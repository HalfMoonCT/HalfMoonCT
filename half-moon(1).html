About

A neighborhood spot that serious food people quietly love.

Half Moon has been a Wallingford staple for years. Chef Stefano runs the kitchen with care — fresh ingredients, handmade dishes, no shortcuts. Stop in for a latte, stay for the pizza.

Family-run · Chef-driven · Since day one
